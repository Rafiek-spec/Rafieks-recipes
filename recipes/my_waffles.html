Waffles

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 Teaspoon salt
  • 4 Teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 Tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 and a half cups of warm milk
  • 1 3rd cup of melted butter
  • 1 Teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients and preheat the waffle iron
  2. Mix all the dry ingredients in a bowl
  3. Mix all the wet ingredients in a bowl
  4. Mix the ingredients together and stir untill it forms a batter
  5. Pour batter onto preheated waffle iron and bake either side untill golden brown
