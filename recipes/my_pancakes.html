Pancakes

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • 3 Tablepsoons of sugar
  • Baking powder and baking soda
  • Salt
  • Buttermilk
  • Wholemilk
  • 3 Whole eggs
  • Melted butter

Steps

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients in a dry bowl
  2. Mix all the wet ingredients in a seperate bowl
  3. Mix both set of ingredients in a bowl now and mix it untill it forms a batter then pour it onto a hot preheated pan and bake your pancakes untill golden brown on each side
