Toast

Ingredients

  • French bread cut into diagonal pieces
  • 8 Large eggs
  • Milk and half and half to complete the custard
  • Vanilla and Cinnamon
  • Butter
  • Brown sugar and corn syrup

Steps

  1. Arrange the bread in a buttered baking dish
  2. Beat the eggs,cream,vanilla,milk and cinnamon togther
  3. Refrigerate overnight
  4. Combine the remaining ingredients in a saucepan and heat it untill it bubbles
  5. Pour the brown sugar mixture over the bread
  6. Bake in a preheated oven
